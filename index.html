💡 Smart Optimization Tips

  • Upgrade to BLDC motors in fans to instantly save up to 60% power.
  • Shift heavy grid loads (AC, Geysers) to solar peak hours (11 AM - 3 PM).
  • Eliminate Phantom Load by switching off idle smart appliances from main plugs.

⚛️ Physics of Power Saving

Ohmic Loss Minimization:

By upgrading to higher gauge copper wiring, we decrease Resistance, reducing heat loss during transmission.

Power Factor Correction:

Using capacitor banks counters inductive loads from motors, bringing the power factor closer to 1.0 for absolute efficiency.

How Modern Cities Manage Electricity Efficiently

Smart Grids

Using IoT sensors to automatically redirect power from low-demand zones to high-demand zones instantly.

Auto-Dimming Streetlights

LDR and motion sensors dim streetlights to 20% power when no traffic or pedestrians are detected.

Peak Load Shaving

Industrial sectors are incentivized to utilize power during midnight troughs rather than afternoon peaks.
